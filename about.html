Who We Are

Built to build.

Relay▸Launch exists because too many small businesses and startups get advice when what they actually need is infrastructure. We close that gap.

The Short Version

We started this because the gap was obvious

Small businesses don't need another consultant who shows up with a slide deck, talks for an hour about "digital transformation," and then sends an invoice. They need someone who actually builds the thing. Someone who sets up the website, configures the automations, designs the workflows, and hands it over working.

That's what Relay▸Launch does. We're a hands-on consulting practice that combines real operational experience with modern platforms and tooling to deliver infrastructure that businesses can use immediately. Not eventually. Not after another round of planning. On delivery day.

We work with two kinds of businesses: small businesses that need marketing lift and digital infrastructure, and startups that need operational systems — ERP migrations, financial workflows, process architecture. Both get the same standard of work. Both get systems they own when we're done.

Our Approach

How we actually get things done

We've built our delivery process around a few principles that let us move faster and build better than what most businesses expect from consulting. This isn't about cutting corners — it's about using the right tools and frameworks to deliver expert-level work on practical timelines.

  • We scope before we start. Every engagement begins with a clear understanding of what you need, what's realistic, and what the deliverables look like. No scope creep. No surprises.
  • We use structured methodologies. Our build process follows proven frameworks that keep projects organized, phases clear, and stakeholders informed. Every project has defined milestones, deliverables, and checkpoints.
  • We leverage modern tooling aggressively. From development platforms to automation builders to infrastructure tools — we use the best available technology to deliver faster without sacrificing quality. This is how we keep timelines tight and output sharp.
  • We document everything. Every system, workflow, and process we build comes with documentation your team can follow. When we leave, your people run it — not us.
  • We stay accountable. Regular check-ins, transparent progress updates, and clear communication throughout. You always know where things stand.
What Drives Us

Principles, not platitudes

Ship it

Plans are only valuable when they turn into working systems. We bias toward action and delivery over endless strategy cycles.

Own the outcome

We don't disappear after handoff. We make sure what we built works, your team can run it, and you're set up for what comes next.

Keep it practical

We build what you need right now — not what might be useful in two years. The right solution is the one that works today and scales when you need it to.

Be straight

If something doesn't make sense for your business, we'll tell you. If there's a better way, we'll recommend it — even if it means less work for us.
