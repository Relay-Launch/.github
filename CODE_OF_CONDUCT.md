# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Relay▸Launch is committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone who interacts with our projects, whether as a client, collaborator, or contributor.

## Standards

We expect all participants to:

- **Be respectful** — Treat others as you would want to be treated
- **Be constructive** — Focus on what is best for the project and the community
- **Be professional** — Keep interactions productive and on-topic
- **Be inclusive** — Welcome diverse perspectives and experiences

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following are not tolerated:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Disrespectful or demeaning language
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **hello@relaylaunch.com**. All reports will be reviewed and addressed promptly.

Project maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
