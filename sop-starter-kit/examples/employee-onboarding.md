## Purpose

Define the standard process for onboarding new employees from offer acceptance through their first 90 days. This ensures every new hire has a consistent, welcoming experience and is set up for success.

---

## Scope

Applies to all new full-time and part-time employees. Contractors follow a modified version (see SOP-HR-003).

---

## Roles & Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| **Hiring Manager** | Initiates onboarding, assigns buddy, conducts role-specific training |
| **Operations Manager** | Coordinates logistics, accounts, and equipment |
| **IT / Admin** | Sets up accounts, devices, and access permissions |
| **New Hire's Buddy** | Provides informal support, answers day-to-day questions |
| **New Employee** | Completes required paperwork, attends orientation, follows the 30/60/90 plan |

---

## Prerequisites

- Signed offer letter received
- Start date confirmed
- Background check cleared (if applicable)
- Manager has confirmed team readiness

---

## Procedure

### Phase 1: Pre-Start (1-2 weeks before Day 1)

1. **Send welcome email** with start date, location/remote instructions, dress code, parking info, and what to bring on Day 1
2. **Create employee record** in HR system (Gusto, BambooHR, or equivalent)
3. **Order equipment** — laptop, monitor, peripherals per role requirements
4. **Set up accounts:**
   - Email (Google Workspace or Microsoft 365)
   - Slack / Teams
   - Project management tool (Asana, Monday.com, etc.)
   - Any role-specific tools (CRM, design software, etc.)
5. **Prepare workspace** — desk, supplies, welcome kit (if in-office)
6. **Schedule Day 1 agenda** — orientation, team lunch, 1:1 with manager
7. **Assign onboarding buddy** from the same team
8. **Share pre-reading materials** — company handbook, org chart, team wiki

### Phase 2: Day 1

1. **Welcome meeting** with manager (30 min)
   - Company mission and values overview
   - Team structure and who does what
   - Expectations for the first week
2. **IT setup walkthrough** — verify all accounts work, configure devices
3. **Office tour / virtual workspace tour** — where things are, key Slack channels, shared drives
4. **HR paperwork** — tax forms, direct deposit, benefits enrollment, emergency contacts
5. **Team lunch** — informal, no agenda, just meet people
6. **Buddy introduction** — 30-minute casual conversation
7. **End of day check-in** with manager — questions, comfort level, anything missing

### Phase 3: Week 1

1. **Daily 15-minute standup** with manager (just for Week 1)
2. **Complete all compliance training** — security awareness, harassment prevention, data handling
3. **Review SOPs** relevant to their role
4. **Shadow a teammate** on a real task or project
5. **Set up recurring 1:1** with manager (weekly, 30 min)
6. **Buddy check-in** — mid-week, informal

### Phase 4: Days 8-30 (First Month)

1. **Assign first real task or project** — something achievable with clear deliverables
2. **Weekly 1:1 with manager** — progress, blockers, questions
3. **Complete role-specific training** modules
4. **30-day check-in meeting** — formal review of adjustment, early feedback
   - What's going well?
   - What's confusing or unclear?
   - Do they have what they need?
5. **Buddy relationship** continues informally

### Phase 5: Days 31-90

1. **Increase responsibility** — independent ownership of tasks
2. **Bi-weekly 1:1s** with manager (can reduce from weekly)
3. **60-day check-in** — performance trajectory, any concerns
4. **90-day review** — formal evaluation
   - Meeting expectations? Exceeding? Struggling?
   - Goals for next quarter
   - Decision: confirm employment, extend probation, or address issues
5. **Onboarding officially closes** — remove from onboarding tracking, transition to regular performance cycle

---

## Exceptions

- **Remote employees:** Replace office tour with virtual workspace walkthrough. Ship equipment at least 5 business days before start date.
- **Senior hires:** Shorten some training modules. Add meetings with cross-functional leads in Week 1.
- **Rehires:** Skip compliance training if completed within the last 12 months.
