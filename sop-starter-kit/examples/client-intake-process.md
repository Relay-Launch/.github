## Purpose

Define the standard process for handling inbound leads from first contact through signed agreement. This ensures every potential client gets a consistent experience and no leads fall through the cracks.

---

## Scope

Applies to all inbound inquiries — website forms, email, referrals, and social media messages. Outbound prospecting follows a separate process (see SOP-SAL-002).

---

## Roles & Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| **Business Development Lead** | Owns the intake pipeline, conducts discovery calls, sends proposals |
| **Operations Manager** | Reviews scope feasibility, confirms capacity |
| **Account Manager** | Takes over after signed agreement for project kickoff |

---

## Prerequisites

- CRM system is set up and accessible
- Intake form or issue template is live and collecting submissions
- Proposal template is ready
- Calendar booking link is configured

---

## Procedure

### Step 1: Lead Received (Day 0)

1. New inquiry arrives via intake form, email, or referral
2. **Log in CRM** — create contact record with:
   - Name, email, phone
   - Business name and industry
   - Source (form, referral, social, etc.)
   - Initial notes on what they need
3. **Set pipeline stage** to "New Lead"
4. **Auto-response sent** (if via form) confirming receipt — see welcome workflow
5. **Assign owner** — Business Development Lead

### Step 2: Initial Qualification (Within 24 hours)

1. Review the submission details
2. **Quick qualification check:**
   - Is this a real business inquiry? (not spam, not a vendor pitch)
   - Is the request within our service scope?
   - Are there any immediate disqualifiers? (budget mismatch, out-of-scope industry)
3. **If qualified:** Move to Step 3
4. **If not qualified:** Send polite decline email, mark as "Closed - Not Qualified" in CRM, log reason

### Step 3: Discovery Outreach (Within 24-48 hours)

1. Send personalized email:
   - Reference their specific challenge or question
   - Propose a 30-minute discovery call
   - Include calendar booking link
   - Suggest 2-3 available time slots as alternatives
2. **Update CRM stage** to "Discovery Scheduled" once booked
3. If no response after 48 hours, send one follow-up
4. If no response after second attempt (5 days total), mark as "Closed - No Response"

### Step 4: Discovery Call (30 minutes)

**Agenda:**
1. **(5 min)** Introduction — who we are, how we work
2. **(15 min)** Learn about their business:
   - What does your business do?
   - What's the main challenge you're facing right now?
   - What have you already tried?
   - What tools and systems are you currently using?
   - What does success look like for you?
   - What's your timeline for getting started?
3. **(5 min)** Explain which package might fit and why
4. **(5 min)** Next steps — we'll send a proposal within 3 business days

**After the call:**
1. Update CRM with detailed notes
2. **Update stage** to "Proposal Pending"
3. Confirm internally with Operations Manager that scope is feasible and capacity exists

### Step 5: Proposal (Within 3 business days of discovery)

1. Draft proposal using template, customized to the client's needs:
   - Summary of their situation and goals
   - Recommended package and deliverables
   - Timeline
   - Investment (pricing)
   - Terms and next steps
2. Review internally before sending
3. Send proposal via email with PDF attached
4. **Update CRM stage** to "Proposal Sent"

### Step 6: Follow-Up

1. If no response within 3 business days, send follow-up email
2. If no response within 7 business days, send final follow-up with a clear deadline
3. If no response within 14 business days, mark as "Closed - No Response" and set a 90-day re-engagement reminder

### Step 7: Agreement & Kickoff

1. Client accepts proposal
2. Send agreement/contract for signature (DocuSign, HelloSign, or equivalent)
3. Once signed:
   - **Update CRM stage** to "Won"
   - Send welcome email with next steps and kickoff meeting invite
   - **Hand off to Account Manager** with full CRM notes
   - Create project in project management tool
   - Schedule kickoff call within 5 business days
4. **Onboarding begins** — see client onboarding SOP

---

## Exceptions

- **Referrals from existing clients:** Skip Step 2 qualification — move directly to discovery scheduling. Note the referral source in CRM.
- **Repeat clients:** Skip discovery call if we already understand their business. Go directly to proposal.
- **Rush requests:** Compress the timeline but don't skip steps. Same process, faster execution.

## Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Lead-to-Discovery Rate | > 60% |
| Discovery-to-Proposal Rate | > 75% |
| Proposal-to-Close Rate | > 40% |
| Average Time: Lead to Close | < 14 business days |
| Response Time: First Contact | < 24 hours |
