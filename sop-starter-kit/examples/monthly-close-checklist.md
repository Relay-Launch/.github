## Purpose

Define the standard process for closing the books each month. This ensures financial records are accurate, complete, and reviewed on a consistent schedule so that business decisions are based on real numbers.

---

## Scope

Covers all monthly financial closing activities for the business. Applies to the last business day of each month through the 10th of the following month (close period).

---

## Roles & Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| **Finance Lead / Bookkeeper** | Executes the close process, reconciles accounts, prepares reports |
| **Operations Manager** | Reviews reports, flags discrepancies, approves final numbers |
| **Department Leads** | Submit expense reports, verify department charges by close deadline |

---

## Prerequisites

- Accounting software is up to date (QuickBooks, Xero, or equivalent)
- Bank and credit card feeds are connected and current
- All team members know the expense submission deadline

---

## Procedure

### Phase 1: Pre-Close (Last 3 business days of month)

- [ ] Send reminder to all team members: expense reports and receipts due by end of month
- [ ] Verify all invoices for the month have been sent to clients
- [ ] Confirm all vendor bills received have been entered
- [ ] Check that payroll has been processed and recorded
- [ ] Review any pending purchase orders or commitments

### Phase 2: Transaction Review (Days 1-3 of new month)

- [ ] **Import all bank transactions** — download and categorize any remaining items
- [ ] **Import all credit card transactions** — match receipts, categorize
- [ ] **Review uncategorized transactions** — clear everything; nothing should be left in "Ask My Accountant" or "Uncategorized"
- [ ] **Enter any manual adjustments:**
  - Depreciation entries
  - Prepaid expense amortization
  - Accrued expenses (services received but not yet billed)
  - Deferred revenue adjustments
- [ ] **Process employee expense reports** — verify receipts, approve, reimburse

### Phase 3: Reconciliation (Days 3-5 of new month)

- [ ] **Bank account reconciliation** — match every transaction, verify ending balance matches bank statement
- [ ] **Credit card reconciliation** — match every charge, verify statement balance
- [ ] **Accounts Receivable review:**
  - Which invoices are outstanding?
  - Which are overdue? By how long?
  - Any that need to be written off?
  - Update aging report
- [ ] **Accounts Payable review:**
  - Any bills due that haven't been paid?
  - Any early payment discounts to capture?
  - Update aging report
- [ ] **Inventory reconciliation** (if applicable) — verify counts match system
- [ ] **Payroll reconciliation** — verify payroll register matches accounting entries

### Phase 4: Review & Reporting (Days 5-8 of new month)

- [ ] **Generate Profit & Loss (Income Statement)** for the month
  - Compare to budget — flag variances > 10%
  - Compare to same month last year
  - Note any unusual items
- [ ] **Generate Balance Sheet** as of month-end
  - Verify assets = liabilities + equity
  - Review for any unusual balances
- [ ] **Generate Cash Flow Statement** (or at minimum, review cash position)
- [ ] **Calculate key metrics:**
  - Monthly revenue
  - Gross margin
  - Net profit margin
  - Operating expenses as % of revenue
  - Cash runway (months of operating expenses covered by cash on hand)
  - Accounts receivable days (DSO)
- [ ] **Prepare month-end summary** — 1-page narrative of what happened financially

### Phase 5: Approval & Lock (Days 8-10 of new month)

- [ ] **Finance review meeting** with Operations Manager
  - Walk through P&L, balance sheet, key metrics
  - Discuss any anomalies or concerns
  - Decide on any action items (collections, cost cuts, etc.)
- [ ] **Address any corrections** identified during review
- [ ] **Lock the period** in accounting software (prevent further changes to closed month)
- [ ] **File supporting documents** — bank statements, reconciliation reports, receipt backups
- [ ] **Update financial dashboard / KPI tracker** with final numbers

## Monthly Close Calendar

| Day | Task |
|---|---|
| Last 3 days of month | Pre-close prep, reminders sent |
| Day 1-3 | Transaction import and categorization |
| Day 3-5 | Account reconciliation |
| Day 5-8 | Report generation and metric calculation |
| Day 8-10 | Management review, corrections, period lock |

---

## Exceptions

- **Year-end close:** Additional steps required — annual adjustments, tax provision, audit prep. See SOP-FIN-002.
- **Months with holidays:** Start pre-close one day earlier for each holiday in the close period.
- **New accounting software migration:** First close on new system may take 2-3 extra days. Plan accordingly.
